## 9:25 Comms Checklist (Director Call And Response)

Use this before doors open to confirm team readiness.

### 1) Comms Check First

- [ ] Director: "Comms check. Camera 1?"
- [ ] Camera 1 confirms clear receive and talkback
- [ ] Director: "Camera 3?"
- [ ] Camera 3 confirms clear receive and talkback
- [ ] Director: "Camera 5?"
- [ ] Camera 5 confirms clear receive and talkback
- [ ] Director: "Graphics?"
- [ ] Graphics Producer confirms clear receive and talkback
- [ ] Director: "Stream?"
- [ ] Stream Producer confirms clear receive and talkback

### 2) Camera Readiness

- [ ] Camera 1 ready: framing, focus, brightness, tripod set, operator ready
- [ ] Camera 3 ready: operator active, or locked shot framed and secured
- [ ] Camera 5 ready: signal strengh, batteries full, clear movement path
- [ ] Fixed cameras available (Camera 2, Camera 4, Camera 6 where applicable)

### 3) Graphics Producer Readiness (In-Room)

- [ ] IMAG ProPresenter loaded with today's playlist/order
- [ ] Lyrics ready and can be advanced reliably
- [ ] Countdown video ready to run at 09:59:00 with music
- [ ] Additional required videos/slides queued (Peace, children, communion, notices)
- [ ] Key/fill output path checked on IMAG chain

### 4) Stream Producer Readiness (Stream-Only Assets)

- [ ] Host names/titles entered and ready for Camera 6 segments
- [ ] Stream-only slides ready (welcome/holding, closing, website/email)
- [ ] Stream-only videos queued and sound checked (trailers, recaps, upcoming items)
- [ ] Host cue plan agreed (09:50 start, 09:59:30 wrap)
- [ ] Stream Deck controls for M/E 2 tested and ready
- [ ] Host lighting on
- [ ] Comms to earpieces working and volume checked
- [ ] Sound check for host mics

### 5) Service Timing Call

- [ ] Director confirms key timeline marks with team:
  - 09:30 doors open
  - 09:49 stream starts (holding slide)
  - 09:50 hosts live on Camera 6
  - 09:59:00 countdown starts

  - 10:00:00 service starts
