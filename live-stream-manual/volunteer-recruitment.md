# Volunteer Recruitment

## Short Spoken Announcement

Anyone here a gamer?

We're looking for volunteers to help run our church livestream. If you're comfortable reacting to screens, making quick decisions, and using simple controls, you could be a great fit.

We have three roles: directing cameras, running the screens in the room, and producing the livestream.

If you have experience with video, streaming, or photography that's great—but no experience is needed and we'll train you.

If you are a gamer, put those skills to use and come chat to Ben or Jamie after the service.
