# Graphics / IMAG Producer Role

## Overview

**Location:** Back of the room

**Primary responsibility:** Managing what appears on the in-room screens.

This role runs **IMAG ME** using a Stream Deck and operates **ProPresenter** for lyrics, slides, and visual elements in the room.

## Key Responsibilities

- Control IMAG camera feeds on the Stream Deck
- Operate ProPresenter for songs, readings, and slides
- Coordinate with the Director to ensure the correct visuals appear in the room
- Trigger visual elements such as slides and supporting media

## Equipment Used

- **Stream Deck:** Quick access controls for IMAG ME switching
- **ProPresenter:** Presentation software for lyrics, slides, and media
- **Computer/Monitors:** Operating the IMAG and ProPresenter systems

## Pre-Service Checklist

- [ ] Launch ProPresenter and load the service playlist
- [ ] Check Stream Deck is connected and controlling IMAG ME
- [ ] Verify in-room screens are displaying correctly
- [ ] Review service order and identify all slides/songs needed
- [ ] Test transitions between camera feeds and ProPresenter content
- [ ] Coordinate with Director on IMAG preferences

## During Service

### ProPresenter Operation

1. **Follow the service flow:** Stay one step ahead in the playlist
2. **Timing:** Trigger lyrics and slides at the right moment
3. **Smooth transitions:** Avoid jarring cuts between visual elements
4. **Be ready to adapt:** Services don't always go exactly as planned

### IMAG Control

1. **Coordinate with Director:** The cameras you show in-room should complement what's online
2. **Show engaging content:** Mix camera angles to keep in-room audience engaged
3. **Balance:** Know when to show cameras vs. slides
   - Worship: More camera, less text (except lyrics)
   - Teaching: Balance speaker shots with teaching slides
   - Liturgy: Text/readings when appropriate

### Communication

- Stay in contact with the Director
- Alert Stream Producer if there are technical issues
- Be ready to adjust on the fly

## Skills That Help

- Comfort using computers
- Attention to timing
- Ability to follow the flow of the service

## Common Scenarios

### Song/Worship

- Display lyrics in ProPresenter synchronized with the music
- Show camera feeds between stanzas or during instrumental sections
- Match the energy - subtle transitions for reflective songs, dynamic for upbeat

### Teaching

- Display teaching slides when referenced by speaker
- Show speaker on IMAG when appropriate
- Balance slides with speaker visibility

### Readings/Liturgy

- Display text for congregation participation
- Time text appearance with readers

### Announcements

- Display announcement slides as directed
- May include video playback
