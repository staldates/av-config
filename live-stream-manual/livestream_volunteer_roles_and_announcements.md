# Livestream Team Roles & Volunteer Recruitment

## Overview

Our livestream relies on a small team working together to serve both the congregation in the room and those watching online. The goal of these roles is not broadcast perfection, but clear communication of what God is doing in the service.

We currently organise the team into three core positions: Director, Graphics / IMAG Producer, and Stream Producer. Each role has specific responsibilities, but all work together to create a cohesive experience for both in-person and online audiences. In addition we have 3 camera operators.

---

# Roles

## Director

**Location:** Gallery / control position

**Primary responsibility:** Visual leadership of the service and camera switching.

The Director controls the **Cam Mix M/E (M/E 3)** on the ATEM control panel and coordinates the camera team. This role decides which camera is live and guides camera operators to frame the next shot.

### Key responsibilities

- Switch cameras during the service using the ATEM control panel
- Communicate with camera operators via comms
- Call shots and prepare the next camera
- Coordinate with the Graphics / IMAG Producer so the room screens and cameras work well together
- Maintain awareness of what is happening on stage

### Skills that help

- Quick reactions to what is happening on screen
- Clear communication
- Comfort using control panels or controllers
- Ability to anticipate the next moment in the service

### Good fits might include

- Gamers
- People interested in video production
- People who enjoy leading a small team

---

## Graphics / IMAG Producer

**Location:** Back of the room

**Primary responsibility:** Managing what appears on the in-room screens.

This role runs **IMAG M/E (M/E 1)** using a Stream Deck and operates **ProPresenter** for lyrics, slides, and visual elements in the room.

### Key responsibilities

- Control IMAG camera feeds on the Stream Deck
- Operate ProPresenter for songs, readings, and slides
- Coordinate with the Director to ensure the correct visuals appear in the room
- Trigger visual elements such as slides and supporting media

### Skills that help

- Comfort using computers
- Attention to timing
- Ability to follow the flow of the service

---

## Stream Producer

**Location:** Gallery (right seat)

**Primary responsibility:** Managing the livestream and online experience.

The Stream Producer controls the **Stream M/E (M/E 2)** using a Stream Deck and manages the YouTube stream and streaming hardware (HELO). They also monitor the livestream chat and coordinate with hosts when needed.

### Key responsibilities

- Control the stream output using the Stream Deck
- Start and monitor the YouTube livestream
- Manage the HELO streaming device
- Display slides such as "Starting Soon" and lower thirds using ProPresenter
- Monitor livestream chat and relay information if needed
- Communicate with online hosts

### Skills that help

- Comfort managing several screens at once
- Attention to detail
- Ability to troubleshoot calmly

---

## Camera Operators

**Location:** Around the room - 1 at the back (camera 1), 1 on the side (camera 3), 1 roving by the stage (camera 5)

**Primary responsibility:** Operating cameras to capture the service.

Camera operators frame shots and capture the service from different angles. They follow the Director's instructions to ensure the livestream has a variety of shots and captures key moments on stage, but they also need to use their own initiative to give the director options. This role requires attentiveness to what is happening during the service and quick reactions to capture important moments.

### Key responsibilities

- Operate assigned camera during the service
- Follow the Director's instructions for framing and shots
- Communicate with the Director via comms
- Maintain awareness of what is happening on stage to anticipate shots

### Skills that help

- Comfort operating cameras or similar equipment
- Ability to follow directions and react quickly
- Interest in video production or photography
